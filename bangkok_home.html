Welcome to Bangkok

Welcome to the vibrant city of Bangkok, the heart of Thailand! Whether you’re a first-time visitor or returning traveler, Bangkok offers a unique blend of the old and the new that never fails to impress.

Take a journey through bustling streets filled with warm, friendly people and incredible street food that will delight your senses. Explore the city’s stunning architecture, where soaring modern skyscrapers like the Baiyoke Sky Tower stand proudly alongside ancient landmarks such as the Grand Palace.

Experience the energy and charm of Bangkok — a city full of surprises and unforgettable moments waiting just for you!

Begin with Thai

  • สวัสดี (sa-wat-dee) — Hello               ขอโทษ (kho-thot) — Excuse me
  • ลาก่อน (la-gon) — Goodbye              ช่วยด้วย (chuay-duay) — Help
  • ขอบคุณ (khop-khun) — Thank you    โรงแรม (rong raem) — Hotel
  • ใช่ (chai) — Yes                                 ฉันไม่เข้าใจ (chan mai kao jai) — I don’t understand
  • ไม่ใช่ (mai chai) — No                        ขอให้โชคดี (kho hai chok dee) — Good luck
